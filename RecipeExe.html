Pratham's recipe for maggie

Ingredients

For the recipe :

  • Maggie noodle packet
  • Boiling water
  • Maggie masala packet

Necessary things required :

  • Cooking utensils required
  • Should boil water

Instructions :

  1. Boil The water
  2. Add a maggie to the water
  3. After some time , add maggie masala
  4. Stir for 2 mintues
  5. Ready to serve
